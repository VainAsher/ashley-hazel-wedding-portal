# Guest Guide — Ashley & Hazel Wedding Portal

For **guests** invited to the wedding. Everything you can do with your invite code.

Site: **https://ashley-and.hazel-of-halifax.com**

## Getting in

1. Ashley & Hazel will send you a personal **invite code** (a short code like `AB12CD34EF`).
2. Go to the site — you'll land on a sealed **envelope** with a little wax seal. Tap it
   to open your invitation (a little confetti for the occasion).
3. Type your code and press **Enter**. You'll arrive at your **Dashboard**.

Your session stays logged in on that device (no password to remember). Use **Logout**
in the top bar to sign out. If your code ever doesn't work, ask the couple to re-send it.

On your **first visit**, a quick tip points out the navigation, and a **"How this
works"** link in the footer opens a friendly recap any time you need it.

## What you'll see

The top navigation has: **Dashboard · RSVP · Schedule · Blessings · Dancefloor ·
Gallery** — plus **Stag Do** or **Hen Do** if you've been flagged as part of the
wedding party (see below). A **bell icon** next to your name shows a badge when the
couple have sent you a message.

### Dashboard
Your home page: a personal welcome (it greets you by name), a **countdown** to the
big day, the **key details** — date, ceremony time, and the ceremony & reception
venues — a **Messages** card for anything Ashley & Hazel send you, a little
**checklist** nudging you toward anything you haven't done yet (RSVP, song, photo,
blessing — it disappears once you're all caught up), and quick links to everything
below.

### RSVP — *let them know if you're coming*
- Choose your response: **Accept**, **Decline**, or **Tentative**.
- If you're coming, tell them your **dietary requirements** (allergies etc.) and add a
  **plus-one name** if you have one.
- **Meal choices**: once the couple finalise the menu and open selection, this page
  grows a **meal picker** for you (and your plus-one, if named) — pick from the
  options they've set up. Until then you'll just see the "menu opens nearer the day"
  note and the dietary field.
- Press **Save**. You can come back and **change it** any time while RSVP is open.
- **If RSVP isn't open yet**, you'll see a friendly message instead of the form — the
  couple haven't opened responses (the wedding is still in "planning"). Check back later.

### Schedule
The plan for the day(s) — the ceremony plus each event, with dates, times, locations,
and any notes, in order. Read-only.

### Blessings — *leave a message for the couple*
- Write a short well-wish and submit it. Your name is filled in for you.
- You'll also see the **wall of blessings** from other guests.
- Note: the couple can moderate messages, so there may be a brief delay or a message
  may be hidden if needed.

### Dancefloor — *help build the wedding soundtrack*
- **Request a song**: title, artist, an optional Spotify/YouTube link, and an optional
  **dedication** (a line about why — dedications appear publicly on the song wall).
- Requests go to Ashley & Hazel for approval; approved songs appear on the **song wall**
  for everyone to see, and you can **♥ react** to your favourites.
- Once a song has a matched preview, a **jukebox** plays a 30-second clip of the
  approved playlist on loop, and whatever the couple mark as **"Currently playing"**
  shows in its own card at the top — handy on the day itself.

### Gallery — *photos*
- Browse the **approved photos** — click any photo to view it **full size**, use the
  arrow keys to move through, or press play for a **slideshow**.
- You can **submit your own photo** (e.g. from the day) — it goes to the couple for
  approval before it appears for everyone. Photos only, up to 25 MB (no video yet).

### Stag Do / Hen Do — *if you're part of the wedding party*
Only visible if Ashley or Hazel have flagged you into their party. A private space for
that party to plan: **event details**, the **members list**, and a **message board** to
coordinate — none of it visible to the couple by default, or to the other party. The
**Best Man** (Stag) or **Maid of Honour** (Hen) can pin important messages, hide
off-topic ones, and edit the details card.

## Spotted something, or have an idea?
A small **💬 Feedback** button sits in the corner of every page — tap it any time to
flag a bug or suggest an idea. It goes straight to Ashley & Hazel.

## Your privacy
The details you enter (name, RSVP, dietary requirements, plus-one) are visible only to
**Ashley & Hazel and their coordinators** — not to other guests. Other guests only ever
see: your name on a blessing if you post one, your name and dedication on an approved
song request, and approved gallery photos. If you're in the wedding party, your Stag/Hen
messages stay inside that party — not visible to the other party, and not to the couple
unless the non-subject partner chooses to reveal it (see below).

## Quick FAQ
- **I can't see the RSVP form.** RSVP isn't open yet — the couple will open it closer
  to the day.
- **Can I bring a plus-one?** Only if your invite allows it; add their name in the RSVP
  form's plus-one field.
- **I made a mistake on my RSVP.** Just open RSVP again and re-save — it updates.
- **Where do I pick my meal?** Not open yet — the couple will enable meal selection
  once the caterer's menu is confirmed (around 12 weeks before the day); once it is,
  a picker appears right on the RSVP page.
- **My song hasn't appeared on the wall.** Requests show up once Ashley & Hazel
  approve them.
- **Why can't I see the Stag/Hen Do link?** Either you're not flagged into that party,
  or — if you *are* one of the couple — that party hasn't been revealed to you yet
  (deliberate, to protect the surprise; your partner or the party's Best Man/Maid of
  Honour controls that).
- **On my phone?** It works the same; the menu is along the top (wraps onto a second
  row if there's a lot of it).
